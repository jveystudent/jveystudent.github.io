About John Francis Vey

John Francis Vey is a New Hampshire photographer, artist, designer, U.S.M.C. Veteran and accidental web site developer who has an Associates Degree in Software Development from Nashua Community College who is currently attending the University of New Hampshire to get his Bachelors in Computer Informations Systems with a minor in Enterpreneurship.

He is often caught on campus muttering to younger students "I wouldn't do that if I were you" while wearing one of two ridiculously long and very distinctive scarves.
